Project Overview

This Excel dashboard provides an analytical view of sales transactions from 2003 to 2005. Built using pivot-based architecture, it allows users to slice data by year, country, customer, quarter, and product line.
The project demonstrates Excel’s capabilities as an entry-level BI tool, replicating real business scenarios involving sales monitoring, customer segmentation, and international revenue analysis.

Key Features
1. Product Performance Analysis

Total revenue per product line

Percentage contribution to total sales

Ranking visualization with bar charts

Average unit price (PRICEEACH) comparison

2. Time-Series Revenue Dashboard

Annual sales for 2003, 2004, and 2005

Quarterly trendline analysis

Slicers for month, quarter, and year

Interactive timeline

3. Customer Insights

Total revenue and quantity purchased per client

Full ranking of customers

Top 10 clients summary (≈30% of sales)

4. Geographic Revenue Performance

Revenue by country and global share

Country-level bar chart

Excel Map Chart for geographic distribution

Filters for country and product line

5. Order Status Monitoring

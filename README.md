Project Overview
This Excel dashboard provides an analytical view of sales transactions from 2003 to 2005. Built using pivot-based architecture, it allows users to slice data by year, country, customer, quarter, and product line. The project demonstrates Excel’s capabilities as an entry-level BI tool, replicating real business scenarios involving sales monitoring, customer segmentation, and international revenue analysis.

Key Features

Product Performance Analysis: Total revenue per product line, percentage contribution to total sales, ranking visualization with bar charts, and average unit price (PRICEEACH) comparison.

Time-Series Revenue Dashboard: Annual sales for 2003–2005, quarterly trendline analysis, slicers for month/quarter/year, and interactive timeline.

Customer Insights: Total revenue and quantity purchased per client, full customer ranking, and Top 10 clients summary representing ~30% of sales.

Geographic Revenue Performance: Revenue by country, percentage share, bar chart by market, Excel Map Chart for geographic distribution, and filters for country/product line.

Order Status Monitoring: Count of order statuses by product line (Shipped, In Process, Cancelled, Resolved, On Hold, Disputed), grouped through PivotTables.

Dataset Overview
The dataset includes order details (quantity, price, sales, order number), date hierarchy (year, quarter, month), product line information, customer details (address, region, territory), and order status. Data cleaning steps included removing inconsistencies, standardizing date formats, creating calculated fields, and structuring a pivot-ready dataset.
